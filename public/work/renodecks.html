What this build proves

Shows home-service websites can feel polished without sacrificing trust or lead-gen clarity.

Audience

Homeowners comparing multiple contractors and looking for proof quickly

Primary win

Scope clarity and project proof show up before the quote ask

Best-fit proof

A local service site that feels premium without losing practicality

The challenge

What needed to feel better immediately

Home-service sites often look interchangeable, which makes trust and differentiation harder when buyers are comparing multiple providers quickly.

Approach

The decisions that shaped the experience

Put proof and scope clarity closer to the top of the journey so visitors understand what the business does well sooner.

Use a more polished visual system than the category norm while keeping the interface straightforward and practical.

Support quote intent with clearer structure instead of relying on generic contractor-site patterns.

  • More polished contractor-brand presentation
