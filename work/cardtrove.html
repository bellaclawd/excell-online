CardTrove.io

A Pokemon card portfolio tracker built for collectors who want cleaner oversight of their collection, market pricing, and day-to-day product workflows.

Collector SaaS Web AppPortfolio TrackingPokemon TCG
Visit live project Start a project like this
What this build proves

Shows Excell can shape a niche collector tool into a clearer SaaS experience with stronger hierarchy and mobile readability.

Audience

Collectors tracking cards, pricing, and future marketplace activity

Primary win

Cleaner hierarchy under heavy product and pricing data

Best-fit proof

A niche SaaS experience that still feels polished on mobile

The challenge

What needed to feel better immediately

Collector products can become visually noisy fast when pricing, grading support, collection data, and future marketplace features all compete for attention.

Approach

The decisions that shaped the experience

Anchor the interface around the collection first, then layer portfolio value, card details, and actions in a way that still feels approachable.

Keep product workflows clean enough for repeat use, especially on mobile where collectors need quick scanning instead of dense dashboards.

Balance hobby energy with enough restraint that the product still feels polished, modern, and trustworthy.

What shipped

Scope at a glance

  • Product UX direction
  • Information hierarchy refinement
  • Responsive app-interface polish
  • Brand-aware visual system tuning

Why this case study matters

  • A stronger example of product UX, not just marketing-page design
  • Shows mobile-friendly handling of image-heavy portfolio tracking
